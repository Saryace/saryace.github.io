üé§ Conferences

This year, 2025, I attended many conferences. I started with SSSA Soil Physics Fukushima, Japan, where I learned about the role of soil in the 2011 disaster. Then I participated in SUITMA25 in Pisa, Italy, where I presented preliminary data on air temperature and heat exposure in urban soils. I won an award for the best presentation on the topic! In Chile, I attended the Chilean Society of Soil Sciences Conference, where I shared the development of R packages, and finally I went to the VIII Chilean Public Health Congress.
